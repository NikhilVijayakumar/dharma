# BAVANS Education Track Governance

## Purpose
Enable product-safe planning, review, and escalation for story-driven educational content across age groups 5-13.

## Activation Triggers
- New educational concept enters story or poem production.
- Existing educational content is repurposed to audio or video channels.
- Age-group mapping is ambiguous or missing.

## Inputs
- Product educational track metadata from product-details.
- Company non-negotiables related to age appropriateness and trust.
- Draft content summary and target audience profile.

## Operating Workflow
1. Identify age band and intended learning objective.
2. Verify concept clarity and emotional integrity requirements.
3. Confirm no cross-track contamination with mature fiction workflows.
4. Require human review sign-off before any publish action.
5. Emit audit note for age-bounded governance traceability.

## Output Contract
- Approved age group assignment and constraints.
- Human-review-required status and reviewer trace.
- Risk trade-off summary for educational integrity.

## Quality Gates
- Strict age-appropriate default behavior.
- No mixing between children's education and mature fiction.
- Human approval is mandatory for production release.
