# Company Non-Negotiables Protocol

- Enforce explicit human approval for production-impacting changes.
- Preserve complete audit trail for critical decisions.
- Block sensitive data exfiltration outside approved channels.
- Require risk trade-off disclosure for every recommendation.
- Prevent autonomous publishing of AI-generated creative outputs.
