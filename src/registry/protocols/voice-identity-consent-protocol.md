# Voice Identity Consent Protocol

- Block any voice or identity replication without explicit consent record.
- Require consent_id, owner_identity, usage_scope, and expiry metadata before activation.
- Prevent cross-track voice reuse between education and mature fiction without human re-approval.
- Escalate consent violations to compliance incident workflow and freeze affected outputs.
- Persist signed consent traces in voice-identity-consent-registry for auditability.
